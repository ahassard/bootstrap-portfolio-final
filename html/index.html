About the Designer

I was born and raised in Dayton, Ohio.  Started college immediately after high school and even had the opportunity to play collegiate softball.  Very soon into my college career I made my way to the local community college and became a certified firefighter/paramedic.  I finally joined the fire service when I was just 19.  After realizing the job market was terrible in Dayton, I made my way to Myrtle Beach, SC.  I continued my career there as a fire/medic.  Taking 20-28 calls in a 24-hour shift took its toll and I began to feel the side effects of burn out.

During that time, I still felt a duty to serve, so I found myself enlisting in the United States Navy at the young age of 26 where I served honorably as an Aviation Electronics Technician making the rank of E-5 (Petty Officer Second Class) in just two years.  I had my ups and downs in the Navy, the best part was getting to see the world.  I lived in Atsugi, Japan for 7 months, deployed and visited Spain, France, Dubai, and Bahrain.  I am very proud of the time I spent in the service.

 Fast forward to the last year and a half where I've gained a lot of knowledge and know-how in web design.  I'm currently taking classes full-time for my web design degree while working a full-time job in avionics.

Long story short, I've learned, trained and have done quite a bit to figure out what I want to be when I grow up.  Some days I still think I have no idea, but right now I'm enjoying everything I'm learning in web design and web development.  Next up; graduation, onto a promising career, starting a family with my wife and then eventually moving on to an undergrad.
